GOLDEN LENS

Celebrating the out of sight aesthetic of the 1970s

ABOUT:

People have been enchanted by the groovy earth tones of the 1970s since they came to be. Disco may be dead, but the aesthetic lives on!

We have the largest of collection of well-preserved vintage 70's era decor and fashion in the country.

Our mission is to share these treasures with people who have a passion for the funky feel of the era, both young and old alike.

We travel all over the country year-round. Come relive the Age of Aquarius with us!
