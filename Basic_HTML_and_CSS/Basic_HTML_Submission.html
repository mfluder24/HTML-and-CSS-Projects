The X-Files originally premiered on September 10, 1993. It aired on the Fox network on Friday nights for its first three seasons.
The show became hugely popular in the 90's and early 2000's, and attracted a large cult following of dedicated fans.
Hardcore fans of the show, known as 'X-Philes', have long studied the in-depth conspiracies and have a wide net of theories pertaining to the many story arcs throughout the program.
The original run of the series ended in May of 2002, but two special Event Seasons 10-11 aired in 2016 and 2018, respectively.
