Mykiss Mists

About

Mykiss Mists is a Rainbow Trout Sanctuary, Educational Center, and campground. Our name comes from our favorite fishy friend, Oncorhynchus mykiss, commonly known as the Rainbow Trout!
Here at Mykiss we focus on education, appreciation, and fun! We have a full staff of USFWS and USDA Forest Service Rangers to help guide and educate people of all ages about the beauty and importance of the outdoors.
We love and protect our finned friends here, and do not allow fishing on-site.
For more information on our educational resources, events, or camping availability please visit our contact section.
